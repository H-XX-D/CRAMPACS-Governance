# crampacs-gen / CRAMPACS-GEN Domain Governance Printable

**Domain:** Genomics and omics

## Assurance Split

- `crampacs-gen`: one to two day preflight.
- `CRAMPACS-GEN`: full assurance system after protocol lock.

## Coordinates

- locus
- variant
- gene
- pathway
- cell type
- tissue
- expression threshold
- perturbation

## Nulls and Non-Events

- failed replications
- non-significant loci
- negative functional assays
- tested pathways not enriched

## Gotchas

- population stratification
- batch effects
- genome-build drift
- winner's curse
