# Document Control Procedure

**Document ID:** CRAMPS-DOC-001  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Draft procedure  
**Owner:** CRAMPS method owner  
**Approver:** accountable release authority or delegated quality owner  
**Effective date:** not effective until approved  
**Review cadence:** at least annually, and after any Critical deviation  

## 1. Purpose

This procedure controls CRAMPS program documents, templates, registers, reports, and release packages.

An organization may not claim an effective CRAMPS quality system until it assigns owners, approvers, effective dates, and review records to the documents it uses.

## 2. Controlled Document Header

Every controlled document must record:

- document ID
- title
- version
- status: draft, approved, retired, superseded
- owner
- approver
- effective date
- review due date
- supersedes
- superseded by
- change summary

## 3. Approval

Draft documents may guide pilots, but approved documents are required for regulated or safety-relevant use.

Approval requires:

- owner review
- quality or method review
- domain/safety review where applicable
- release authority approval for externally used procedures

## 4. Change Control

Changes are classified as:

- administrative: typo, formatting, path correction
- minor: clarification that does not change a control or evidence requirement
- major: changes control requirements, gates, authority, evidence, statistics, or claim limits
- emergency: required to prevent unsafe or materially misleading use

Major and emergency changes require:

- version increment
- change summary
- impacted-documents list
- approval record
- communication to active study owners

## 5. Obsolete Documents

Retired or superseded documents must be:

- marked obsolete
- removed from active templates
- retained in archive
- excluded from new study packages

## 6. Document Register

The controlled document register is `program/registers/document_register.csv`.

No document is effective unless it appears in the register with status `approved`.
