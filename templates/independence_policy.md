## 1. Purpose

## 2. Dependence Sources

Assess shared raw data, instrument, site, vendor, collaboration, calibration, pipeline, simulation, public release, reporting source, or theory-fashion target.

## 3. Grades

| grade | meaning | primary analysis treatment |
|---|---|---|
| A | Independent instrument, team, data, calibration, and analysis logic | Eligible as independent |
| B | Independent data with partially shared theory or calibration | Eligible with note or weight |
| C | Same facility/collaboration but distinct channel | Clustered or down-weighted |
| D | Same dataset or pipeline, derivative evidence | Not counted independently |
| E | Duplicate or non-independent | Excluded or collapsed |
